Rules & Guidelines


GENERAL GUIDELINES


  • Residents should take particular care not to bring any visitor beyond the reception located at the ground floor.
  • All residents should be normally back in the hostel by 7.30 p.m. In the pursuit of their academic work, they may stay in their college premises till 9.00 p.m., only with prior permission from hostel warden.
  • If a resident wishes to stay out after 7:30 p.m. for any other purpose, he/she has to take prior permission of the Warden.


  • Playing football, cricket, etc. in the hostel corridors and gardens between the wings is strictly banned.
  • Students are not allowed to be outside the hostel after 9.00 p.m. for any reason without prior approval of Warden.
  • It is mandatory for all the residents to mark evening attendance in the attendance register. Residents failing to do so will be considered absent from the hostel without permission.
